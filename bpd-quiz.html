About This Assessment

This quiz is designed to help you assess whether you might have traits associated with Borderline Personality Disorder (BPD). The questions are based on common symptoms of BPD as outlined in diagnostic criteria.

Important Disclaimer: This quiz is not a diagnostic tool. It cannot determine with certainty whether you have BPD. Only a qualified mental health professional can make an accurate diagnosis after a proper clinical assessment. If you're concerned about your mental health, please consult a healthcare provider.

The quiz takes approximately 5 minutes to complete. Please answer each question honestly for the most accurate results.
